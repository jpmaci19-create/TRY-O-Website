Our story

TRY-O is recharging the future of urban mobility.

Founded by design-obsessed engineers and street culture aficionados, TRY-O merges performance technology with monochrome aesthetics. We create rechargeable chargers that empower creators and commuters to roam freely.

From night rides to global drops

TRY-O started as a passion project in Brooklyn and has grown into a global drop shipping hub for next-gen charging solutions.

2018

Concept sparks

Co-founders sketch the first TRY-O power bank inspired by subway tile patterns and streetwear minimalism.

2019

Prototype to product

We partner with certified manufacturers and launch the first TRY-O limited run online.

2021

Global drop shipping network

TRY-O expands to EU and Asia with localized fulfillment and sustainability partners.

2023

Urban charging ecosystem

We introduce modular accessories, solar integrations, and collaborative design drops with local artists.
